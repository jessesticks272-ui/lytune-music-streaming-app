Privacy

How Lytune handles your data

This page gives the app a real privacy destination instead of a dead link. It can be expanded later with final legal wording, but it already explains the main user expectations in a clean branded format.

Account information

Lytune may store your name, email address, profile photo, and saved preferences so your account feels consistent across sessions.

Listening activity

Lytune may use listening history, liked items, downloads, and search behavior to improve recommendations and personalize your experience.

Third-party providers

If you sign in with Google or browse external content providers, some information may be shared only as needed to complete that action.

Your controls

You can update your display name, profile image, and future account settings from inside Lytune as more account controls are added.
